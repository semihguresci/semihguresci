Privacy Policy

How this website handles visitor information and communication data.

This website is a personal portfolio and blog operated by Semih Güreşçi. It is designed to be informational and does not require account registration, user logins, or direct form submission.

Information Collected

This website does not intentionally collect personal data through on-site forms or user accounts. If you choose to contact me by email or through third-party platforms such as LinkedIn or GitHub, the information you share is provided by you voluntarily.

Technical Data

Hosting providers and platform services may process standard technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, and access timestamps in server logs for security, reliability, and traffic delivery purposes.

Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use advertising trackers or profiling cookies. A local theme preference may be stored in your browser so the site can remember light or dark mode between visits.

Third-Party Links

This website links to third-party services including GitHub and LinkedIn. Those services operate under their own privacy policies and terms.

Contact
