Data Policy

What data may be processed, why it is used, and how long it may be retained.

Purpose of Processing

Any data related to this website is used only for core site operation, security, delivery, and direct professional communication initiated by visitors.

Data Sources

Data may come from standard server logs, email communication, and interactions on external platforms linked from this website. No on-site account database or customer relationship system is maintained here.

Retention

Technical log data is retained only as long as required by the hosting environment. Emails and professional messages may be retained as needed to respond to inquiries, maintain ongoing communication, or keep a record of relevant professional correspondence.

Sharing

Personal data is not sold or shared for advertising purposes. Data may be processed by infrastructure or platform providers strictly as part of hosting, email delivery, source-code hosting, or professional networking services.

Your Choices

You can choose not to contact me through email or third-party platforms and can clear locally stored theme preferences through your browser settings at any time.

Updates

This policy may be updated if the website's functionality, tooling, or data handling changes.
